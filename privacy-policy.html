Privacy Policy

Interlude : Nature Sleep Sound  |  Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

Interlude is developed and operated by Gentlespace.labs. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal information on our own servers. However, the third-party services integrated into the app collect certain data as described below.

3. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Collected
Google AdMob Displaying ads Advertising ID, device info, ad interaction data
Firebase Analytics App usage analytics App events, device info, session data
Firebase Crashlytics Crash reporting Crash logs, device state at time of crash
Firebase Authentication User accounts Email address (if you sign in)
Google Play Billing In-app purchases Purchase confirmation (handled entirely by Google)

All of the above services are operated by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. App Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

  • INTERNET — required to load ads, sync Firebase, and stream media content
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — keeps audio playing while the app is in the background
  • WAKE_LOCK — prevents the device sleeping during active audio sessions
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — sends optional renewal reminders (only if you opt in)

The app does not request access to your Location, Camera, Microphone, or Contacts.

5. In-App Purchases

All purchases (6-month plan and Lifetime plan) are processed entirely by Google Play. Gentlespace.labs does not receive or store your payment information. Only purchase confirmation status is used by the app to unlock features.

6. Data Retention

Gentlespace.labs does not operate independent servers that store your personal data. Analytics and crash data is retained according to Google Firebase's data retention policies.

7. Children

Interlude is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same menu.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
