Data Deletion Request

Interlude : Nature Sleep Sound  |  Gentlespace.labs

Your Right to Delete Your Data

You can request the deletion of your account and any associated data at any time. We will process your request within 7 business days.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to us with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • The email address associated with your Interlude account
  • A brief note confirming you want your data deleted

Send your request to:

gentlespace.labs@zohomail.com

What Gets Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  • Delete your Firebase Authentication account
  • Request deletion of any associated analytics data from Firebase
  • Remove any account-linked data stored within the app

What We Cannot Delete

Some data may be retained by third-party services beyond our control, such as aggregated and anonymised analytics data held by Google. For more information, see Google Firebase's privacy policy.

Purchase history is managed entirely by Google Play and cannot be deleted by us. To manage that, visit your Google Play account.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at gentlespace.labs@zohomail.com.
